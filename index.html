What is Christian Science?

Established in 1879 by Mary Baker Eddy, the Christian Science Church (also known as The Church of Christ, Scientist) is a Christian denomination based on Jesus' teachings. The Bible, and particularly the New Testament, form the foundation of its theology and practice.

Christian Science emphasizes the healing power of prayer and the spiritual nature of God and humanity.
