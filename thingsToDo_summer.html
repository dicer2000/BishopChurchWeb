Our Favorite Summer Things to Do in Bishop, California

Hikes

  • Bishop Creek Canyon Trails - Explore stunning trails in Bishop Creek Canyon with access to Pacific Crest Trail
  • North Lake Trailhead - Popular starting point for John Muir Wilderness
  • Buttermilk Country Trails - Miles of mountain biking trails with scenic views

Summer Activities

  • Lake Sabrina - Boating, kayaking, paddleboarding, and swimming with boat rentals available
  • Bishop Creek Fishing - Stocked streams perfect for fly fishing and spin fishing
  • Mountain Biking - Extensive trail system in Buttermilk Country
  • Hiking Trails - From easy walks to challenging Sierra Nevada peaks
  • Lake Relaxation - Swimming and picnicking at beautiful mountain lakes

Local Dining

  • Pizza Factory - Casual dining in Bishop
  • Amigo's Mexican Restaurant - Authentic Mexican cuisine in Bishop
  • Back Alley Bowling & Steakhouse - Family-friendly restaurant with great steaks
  • Erick Schat's Bakery - Fresh baked goods and coffee

Summer Itinerary

Thursday
  • Explore Bishop Creek Canyon trails
  • Visit Lake Sabrina for boating and swimming
  • Fishing at Bishop Creek streams
  • Mountain biking on Buttermilk Country trails
Friday
  • Boating at Lake Sabrina (rentals available)
  • Mountain biking on local trails
  • Evening fishing in Bishop Creek
  • Dinner at Pizza Factory
Saturday
  • Visit Bishop Country Club for golf
  • Shop at Erick Schat's Bakery for fresh bread
  • Relax at Amigo's Mexican Restaurant for dinner
Sunday
  • Up early for breakfast at Jack's
  • Church at 10AM at Christian Science Church in Bishop, CA
  • Goody stop at Schatts Bakery
  • Drive home
  • Home in time for a quick dip in the Pacific!
